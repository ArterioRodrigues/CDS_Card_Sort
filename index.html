Learn More

  • Occupation Odyssey offers insights into your work values,aiding you in discerning the aspects that hold significance for you in a job.
  • It accomplishes this by prompting you to prioritize diverse work elements that encapsulate six pivotal work values. Understanding these
  • values assists you in identifying the types of job roles and career avenues that align with your preferences.


  • Upon completing the Odyssey, you will receive scores for each of the six work values. These scores signify the level of importance each work
  • value holds for you. Moreover, you will be directed to a compilation of occupations associated with your work values. This compilation serves as
  • a guide to explore potential career paths. The greater the harmony between a job and your work values, the higher the likelihood of job satisfaction.
